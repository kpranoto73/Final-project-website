We are Emerald City Boom

Emerald City Boom is a passion project created and lead by the one and only Craig Winter. We are a Pacific Northwest based community drumline that likes to play sick beats and have a lot of fun while doing so. Our main focus is learning, we want our kids to have a solid drumming foundation that they can use as a springboard into their music career. Many of our students are a part of or go on to join the University of Washington Marching Band, as well as drum corps. Every year we grow, this 2019 season we were able to travel to Canada and compete in the British Columbia Provincial Drumline Championships where we took first place in our division.
